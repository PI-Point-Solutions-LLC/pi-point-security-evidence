## Assurance boundary

PI Point uses NIST Cybersecurity Framework 2.0 to organize, assess, and continuously improve its cybersecurity risk-management program. PI Point does not claim NIST certification, ISO 27001 certification, a SOC examination, or independent third-party attestation.

Public source and policy evidence demonstrates documented design and management controls. Production provider settings that cannot be safely made public require redacted screenshots or exports, identified explicitly in the attachment register.
